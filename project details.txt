T14 — Personalised Indian Recommender
The problem. Recommendation systems are the most-used ML technique in industry. Build
a small but real one for an Indian-flavour domain.
What you will build. Streamlit app: query input (text or chips) → ranked card list →
“why?” below each item → “save to favourites”.
Skills. Sentence embeddings, cosine similarity retrieval, LLM justification generation, no training.
Common base. sentence-transformers/all-MiniLM-L6-v2 for embeddings. faiss-cpu or
chromadb for retrieval.
